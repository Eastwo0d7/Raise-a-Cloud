Raise a Cloud

Welcome to Raise a Cloud. An application to heighten your awareness and pique your curiosity.

The form below allows you to search for a word, and a word cloud will be formed with its synonyms, antonyms, words that rhyme, or examples of the word in a sentence. A random word will be taken from this selection, and will be used to search for YouTube videos that get added to the page.

Prepare to get lifted!
